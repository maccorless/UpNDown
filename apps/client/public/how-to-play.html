How to Play UpNDown

UpNDown is a cooperative card game. Everyone wins together or loses together.

Goal

Play all cards from all players onto four foundation piles.

Foundation Piles

  • Two ascending piles start at 1. You normally play higher cards.
  • Two descending piles start at 100. You normally play lower cards.

Special Rule: Backward 10

  • Ascending piles can also take a card exactly 10 lower than the top card.
  • Descending piles can also take a card exactly 10 higher than the top card.

Example: On ascending 47, you may play 57 (normal) or 37 (backward 10).

Turn Basics (Multiplayer)

  1. On your turn, play at least the required minimum cards.
  2. Play by selecting a card and then selecting a valid pile.
  3. Use End Turn only after meeting the minimum.

Solitaire Mode

  • Solitaire is great for practice and learning the pile logic.
  • You still follow legal move rules and backward-10 plays.
  • There is no teammate turn order to coordinate, so play is faster.

Difficulty and Settings

You can tune game difficulty in Settings before a game starts.

  • Hand Size: larger hands are usually easier because you have more choices.
  • Min Cards Per Turn: higher minimums are harder to win with.
  • Card Range: a smaller card range is usually easier to win.
  • Auto Refill Hand: turning this on generally makes the game easier.

Win and Loss

  • Win: all players clear all cards and draw pile is exhausted.
  • Loss: no legal moves remain for the active state.

Quick Strategy

  • Save backward-10 plays when possible.
  • Avoid pushing a pile too far if teammates may need lower or higher ranges.
  • In multiplayer, communicate likely future plays before committing extremes.
